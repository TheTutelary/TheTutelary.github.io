# Desi European: Board of Agents 🏛️

This document defines the governance structure and synergy rituals for the Desi European platform's multi-agent system.

## 👑 Hierarchy
1.  **Design Architect (Lead)**
    - *Direct Reports:* Market Research Specialist, Travel Writer, Design Inspector.
    - *Key Function:* Synthesis, Roadmap, Final Approval.

2.  **Specialist Subordinates**
    - **Market Research:** Trends, Audience, Competitors.
    - **Travel Writer:** Content, Narrative, Emotional Tone.
    - **Design Inspector:** Standards, Compliance, Technical Polish.

---

## 📅 Governance Structure (Meetings)

### 1. The Strategy Session (Weekly)
- **Purpose:** Align the 3-month roadmap with current market trends.
- **Inputs:** Trend report from Market Research.
- **Output:** Approved content themes and feature priorities.

### 2. The Implementation Audit (Per Post)
- **Purpose:** Ensure a new travelogue meets both narrative and design standards.
- **Inputs:** Draft from Travel Writer + Compliance report from Design Inspector.
- **Output:** Final published post + updated Homepage visuals.

---

## ⚡️ Synergy Ritual (How to Invoke)

You can invoke the Board collective by using the following phrase:

> **"Assemble the Board: [Topic/Milestone]"**

### What happens when you invoke:
1.  **Market Research** scans for the latest trends relevant to the topic.
2.  **The Travel Writer** drafts a narrative concept based on those insights.
3.  **The Design Inspector** audits the existing UI/CSS to ensure it can support the concept.
4.  **The Design Architect** synthesizes these three into a final **Strategic Recommendation**.

---

## 🗺️ Shared Roadmap Goals
- **Q2 2026:** Fully automate the "Desi-European" cultural intersection identification in photos.
- **Q3 2026:** Implement dynamic typography that shifts scale based on the "mood" of the travelogue.
- **Q4 2026:** Launch the "Boutique Booking" interface based on Market Research's white-space analysis.
